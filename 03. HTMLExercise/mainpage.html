Forks, Washington

Forks, WA is a small, atmospheric town located in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. It gained prominence as the primary setting of the Twilight Series, according to Stepahine Meyer's novels and subsequent film adapatations. The town is often portrayed as enveloped in eternal mist and perpetual rain, creating a moody and enchanting backdrop of the story.

According to the fictional world of Twilight Saga Series, Forks is known for its lush green forests and misty mountains. The description in the book and films depict Forks as a place with a unique charm and a close-knit community, which adds to its allure. Visitors can explore real-life locations mentioned in the Twilight series, such as Forks High School or the Cullen family residence, to immerse themselves in the world of the story.
